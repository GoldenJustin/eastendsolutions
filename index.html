Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that you may have questions about our products and services, and we're here to provide you with clear and informative answers

What equipment will I need for the internet installation, and is it provided by your company?

The equipment required for your internet installation depends on the type of service you choose. In most cases, we provide antenna, routers and any necessary installation hardware i.e Wi-Fi equipment for in-home/office wireless connectivity.

How accurate is your vehicle tracking system, and how frequently is the data updated?

Our vehicle tracking system is highly accurate and provides real-time data updates. It utilizes advanced GPS technology to track the location of your vehicle with precision. The data is typically updated at short intervals, such as every 30 seconds or less, ensuring you have the most up-to-date information available.

How long does it usually take from when I request internet service?

The time it takes to set up your internet service can vary depending on several factors, including your location, the type of service you're requesting. The overall timeline can vary, but we strive to make the process as efficient as possible to get you connected to the internet quickly.

Is the vehicle tracking system compatible with all types of vehicles, including personal cars and commercial fleets?

Yes, our vehicle tracking system is designed to be versatile and can be used with a wide range of vehicles, including personal cars, trucks, vans, and commercial fleets. It is scalable and can accommodate the tracking needs of both individuals and businesses.

What support options are available if I encounter technical issues with my internet service ?

We offer multiple support channels, including phone support, live chat, and email. Additionally, you can find helpful troubleshooting resources in our "Support" or "FAQ" sections on the website.

How does your car tracking system work, and what are its benefits?

Our car tracking system utilizes advanced technology to provide real-time location tracking and security features for your vehicle. Benefits include theft prevention, improved vehicle safety, and potential cost savings on insurance premiums.
